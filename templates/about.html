What do we support?

This website focuses on Arabic Named Entity Recognition(ANER).There's a lack in Arabic NER tools in general and specifically for Franco(transliterated Arabic). This website is a solution for applying NER on text that is arabic or transliterated arabic or mix between them.

Who are we?

We are 5 Egyptian students in the faculty of engineering at ASU (CSE department). This website starts as a graduation project to provide solutions to the challenges of Arabic language in NER, and add support for Franco! We created this website under the supervision of Prof.Dr.Ahmed Hassan, the dean of Nile university, and Eng.Sara Abdu, TA at ASU university.
